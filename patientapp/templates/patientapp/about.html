About KiddoVax

Helping parents manage vaccinations with confidence and simplicity.

Our Story

Who We Are

KiddoVax was created to solve a critical issue in child healthcare: managing vaccination schedules and digital health records seamlessly. Our platform bridges the gap between pediatric healthcare providers and parents.

By digitizing immunizations, we eliminate the reliance on paper charts that can be lost or damaged. We provide families with automatic age-based notifications, clinical schedule tracking, and detailed vaccine educational guides.

Our commitment to parents is absolute — providing a secure, transparent, and user-friendly portal for your child's health journey.

Our Mission

Mission

Our mission is to make childhood immunizations simpler and stress-free for families. We empower parents with the right tools, knowledge, and secure record tracking to make informed decisions.

Through platform security and clinical partnerships, we aim to increase awareness about the life-saving benefits of timely vaccines and support healthcare ecosystems globally.

Our Vision

Vision

We envision a future where digital healthcare is seamlessly accessible to every family. KiddoVax strives to be the gold standard in childhood immunization tracking, providing parent peace of mind.

By integrating smart technologies and automated notifications, we look forward to achieving 100% vaccination compliance and contributing to a safer, healthier childhood globally.
