Who We Are

Empowering Care Through Innovation

The KiddoVAX involves creating a user-friendly website with the goal of efficiently managing immunizations for youngsters up to the age of 18. The use of RFID technology for accurate data capture during visits will allow parents to easily plan, monitor, and organize immunization sessions.

Parents will be able to view their child's immunization record while the system protects the confidentiality and privacy of their personal information. The website will further give clinic locators, educational resources, contacts with medical specialists, and telemedicine services.

Our initiative seeks to improve child health in general.
