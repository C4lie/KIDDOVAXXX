star Popular Vaccines

Overview of widely administered critical childhood vaccinations.

BCG

Bacille Calmette-Guérin

Administered at birth. Primarily protects infants against tuberculosis (TB) and related complications.

OPV

Oral Polio Vaccine

Administered in multiple doses. Essential for building gastrointestinal immunity against poliomyelitis (polio).

Penta

Pentavalent Vaccine

A combined 5-in-1 vaccine protecting against Diphtheria, Pertussis, Tetanus, Hepatitis B, and Haemophilus Influenzae Type B.

MMR

Measles, Mumps, Rubella

Combined live vaccination administered starting from 9 months. Builds critical defenses against childhood virus outbreaks.
